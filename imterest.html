My Interests

Earlier in my self tab I mentioned some of my interests already, here I'll be going more in to depth on why I'm interested in them.

Video Games
I've been plaing video games for as long as I can remember and longer. In fact, my mom says I started playing games whenever she bought a console for herself with the game Ms.Pac-Man. At that time I was only around two years old and I've been playing games ever since then.

Writing
Ever since I wrote my first poem for class in 8th grade I felt like I really enjoyed writing. For me it was a great way to put my thoughts and feelings out there without necessarily having to express them verbally to the world.

Reading
I think reading and writing kind of go hand in hand; something English teachers say quite often is how to become a better writer you have to becom a better reader and vice versa. Reading for me is just another nice way to pass the time when I don't have much else to do.

Resting
On my self page I also including "resting" as one of the things I like to do in my free time. Honestly that was just me saying "I like to sleep a lot" in a nicer way. Sleeping is a nice way to pass time whenever I'm bored or waiting for something. It also keeps me feeling more rested.
